24MadRush

Made with 24a2

Background

Different shaped pieces will fall from the top of the screen. Fill entire rows with

dots to clear them and gain bonus points. Clear multiple rows with one

piece to get combos. Pieces will fall faster as the game goes on.

Controls

Left Arrow:   Moves piece to the left
Right Arrow:   Moves piece to the left
Down Arrow:   Drops piece to its shadow
Up Arrow:   Rotates piece

On Challenge Mode

Click:   Switches to other piece
